Sausage Balls

Delicious balls of meat and they are super easy to make

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground sausage
  • 1 pack of cream cheese
  • 1 lb grated sharp cheddar
  • 2 cups of waffle mix

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Combine all ingredients and mix well
  3. Grease baking sheet
  4. Take mixed ingredients, ball up into 2 inch spheres, and spread across baking sheet with 1/2 inch between one another
